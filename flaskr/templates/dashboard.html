    Project Description

    The theme of our project is building a Network about LinkedIn Jobs Posting Dataset. First of all, Why we care this network? We do know the significance of employee networking. Based on our analysis of individual career paths datasets, focusing on their working companies, job titles, and member followers. We go as deep as we can to discover the social network connections between them. Eventually, We hope the analysis results will give some insights and suggestions to recent graduates, or people who are planning to change their job, helping them to get a better career development in the future.
